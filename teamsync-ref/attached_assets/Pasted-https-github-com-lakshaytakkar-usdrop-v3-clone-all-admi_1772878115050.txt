🧭 USER JOURNEY FLOW
Think of it as a 5-room house. Every client walks through rooms in sequence.

🚪 Room 1 — Entry (Onboarding)
Client gets access after purchasing the program.

Receives login credentials
Lands on a Welcome Dashboard
Sees their progress tracker (what's done, what's next)
Prompted to complete profile (niche, budget, experience level)


📦 Room 2 — Product Research
This is where they spend the most time early on.

Opens Product Research Engine
Browses winning products (pulled from live data)
Filters by niche, price range, trend score
Views competitor stores selling similar products
Saves shortlisted products to their personal list
Gets AI suggestions on what to sell based on their niche


🏪 Room 3 — Store Setup
After picking a product, they move to store execution.

Follows Store Setup Checklist inside the platform
Gets guided steps (Shopify setup, theme, product page)
Access to creative generation tools (ad creatives, banners)
Can view course modules related to store setup
Submits store for review (if that workflow exists)


📢 Room 4 — Ads & Launch
Once store is ready, they move to running ads.

Access Ad Strategy modules
Use creative generation for Meta/TikTok ads
Follow ad launch checklist
Track ad performance (manual input or integrated)
Get AI-generated copy suggestions


📬 Room 5 — Order Fulfillment & Scaling
When orders come in, the platform helps them not panic.

View Fulfillment SOPs inside the platform
Track order status workflow
Access scaling guides
Get notified on next milestones


🔧 ADMIN FLOW (What Your Team Sees)
Think of it as a control room behind the house.

👁️ Admin View 1 — Client Pipeline

See all active clients and their current stage
Know who is stuck, who is progressing, who needs help
Filter by: stage, join date, LLC status, store status


📋 Admin View 2 — Task & Ticket Management

See support requests raised by clients
Assign tickets to team members
Track resolution status
Flag escalations for Lakshay


📊 Admin View 3 — Content & Course Management

Add/edit modules, lessons, resources
Publish session recordings
Update product database (bestsellers, trending items)
Manage competitor store data


🏢 Admin View 4 — LLC Tracking

See each client's LLC formation stage
Update status (Filed → EIN Received → BOI Done → Bank Setup)
Send automated reminders
Upload documents per client


📈 Admin View 5 — Platform Health

Total active users
Module completion rates
Most-used features
Drop-off points (where clients go sile
